Survive Till NightFall

Survive Till NightFall is a Horror/Survival game where 10 Survivors need to complete certain objectives in order to gain Items which They will need to defeat the 2 killers hunting them. The Survivors will need to hide, communicate and work together or suffer a deadly defeat. This game was made by the company named DragonHRG. Survive Till NightFall offers special bundles which can be purchased with real money.
