**Apex Wings Simulator**



* In this game, you unlocked different style of wings, each with their own special abilities,and buffs to gather coins to upgrade your character and become a master.





**Key Features:**



Biome-Based Progression: Explore diverse environments, starting from the lush Forest Zone to the freezing Tundra, each with unique assets and challenges.



Integrated UI System: Simple Buttons for "Claim Wings," Shop, and Inventory management to ensure a user-friendly experience.



Central Hub Design: A main fountain area serving as the player's home base for social interaction and upgrading.



Simulator Mechanics: Optimized resource collection and pet/item inventory systems (currently in implementation).
